About

I’m Tanya Lupashku — a brand designer and art director with over 20 years of experience shaping brands that think clearly and move with purpose.

My work sits at the intersection of strategy and beauty. I design systems of meaning that help ideas grow, connect, and last. Whether working with founders, studios, or fast-growing teams, the focus is always the same: clarity, coherence, and the quiet power of design that makes sense.

I work independently or assemble a team to fit the project. 1509designs is my creative practice — based in London, working with clients across Europe and the US.
